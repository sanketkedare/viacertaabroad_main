1) Required Pages -
- Home – Overview of services, highlights, and success stories.
- About Us – Company mission, vision, achievements, and team introduction.
- Study Destinations – List of countries where students can study.
- Individual Destination Page – Detailed information about each country.
- Top Universities – Overview of partner universities.
- Individual University Page – Detailed information about each university and its courses.
- Entrance Exams – Overview of exams conducted worldwide.
- Individual Exam Page – Detailed information about each exam, syllabus, dates, and preparation tips.
- Services – Visa assistance, scholarships, accommodation, part-time and full-time jobs.
- Application Process – Step-by-step guide to applying through your company.
- Student Testimonials – Success stories from past students.
- Blog & Updates – Latest news, guides, and insights on studying abroad.
- Student Profile Page – Individual student information and progress tracking.
- Sign-in form
- Student Dashboard (if logged in) – Personalized dashboard for applications, notifications, and status updates.
- Contact Us – Inquiry form, email, phone number, office location, and social media links.
